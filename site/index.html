How to read this book

Every chapter follows one running example — the Repo Assistant — from a single workflow to an organization-wide fleet. The 14 chapters are grouped into three parts that scale up in scope. Two tracks run through the book; follow the call-outs that match your role.

Builder track

You author, compile, and run workflows. Read the parts in order and work every Worked example; watch for Builder call-outs with hands-on detail.

Leader track

You care about safety, cost, governance, and adoption. Skim the Concept and When to use sections and follow the Leader call-outs; Parts II–III are written for you.
